React Native Bible

Privacy Policy

Effective July 11, 2026
Short version: React Native Bible doesn't collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There's no account, no backend, and no analytics. Everything the app does happens locally on your device.

01How the app works

React Native Bible is an offline-first reference app. All content — sections, topics, quizzes, and component references — ships inside the app itself and is stored locally on your device. The app does not communicate with any server, and it has no login, account system, or user profile of any kind.

02Data we collect

None. The app does not collect names, emails, device identifiers, location, usage analytics, crash reports, or any other personal or usage data. Nothing you do inside the app — reading a topic, taking a quiz, changing a setting — is transmitted anywhere.

03Local storage on your device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device using standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage), such as your selected language, theme, and quiz progress. This information never leaves your device, is never sent to us, and is removed automatically if you uninstall the app.

04Third parties

React Native Bible does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking service. No data is shared with affiliates, partners, or service providers, because none are involved in how the app operates.

05Children's privacy

Since the app collects no data from anyone, this includes children. React Native Bible does not knowingly or unknowingly gather personal information from users of any age.

06Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds a feature that involves data collection — this page will be updated and the "Effective" date above will reflect the change.

07Contact

Questions about this policy or the app can be sent to norton_rayan@hotmail.com.

React Native Bible is an open-source project. Source available at github.com/NRayan/ReactNativeBible.
